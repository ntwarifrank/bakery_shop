Congratulations on your Engagement!

Please enjoy our wedding galleries that feature many beautiful cake options to help make your day perfect! From intimate 2 tier cakes, cupcakes, our popular 3 tier Classic Weddings Cakes, we can make something just right for your unique taste.

Allow us the  opportunity to design and create the wedding cake of your dreams. We are happy to answer any questions you might have regarding flavors, pricing, design and more.

Cake Delivery (set up is included with delivery) starts at $150 and is calculated by the distance from the shop in La Place to the venue. Friday and Sunday Deliveries will start at $200.  The Classic Wedding Cake is quite manageable to pick up and deliver yourself.  We will be happy to help safely load the cake into the car or van for you at no extra cost.

Please call our ETON location at (216) 514-2253 to set up your wedding cake tasting and design consultation.

You deserve a stunning wedding cake that tastes incredible.

 
 

Classic Wedding Cakes

Classic Wedding Cakes have become a popular option for our Brides & Grooms over the last few years, especially when planning large scale weddings.  These gorgeous 3 tier round buttercream cakes are a perfect centerpiece for your reception. 2 layer sheet style extra servings cakes or cupcakes are a great way to supplement the cake, in order to reach the amount of servings you will need.

The Classic Wedding Cake is offered in 2 different sized 3 tiers cakes.

A 3 tier to serve 75 starts at $750

A 3 tier that serves 100 starts at $1000.

Extra serving cakes are $4 per serving and gourmet cupcakes start at $3.75 each.
